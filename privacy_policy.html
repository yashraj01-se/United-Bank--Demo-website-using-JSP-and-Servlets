Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to United Bank. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us in any way.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you open an account, apply for a loan, fill out forms on our website, or contact us. The types of information we collect include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Financial information (such as account details)
  • IP address and usage data

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide the services you request, such as processing your account and providing customer support. We may also use your information to:

  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you important updates or notices regarding your account
  • Notify you about promotions or special offers, unless you opt out

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include encryption and firewalls to secure sensitive information such as financial data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us via the details provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
